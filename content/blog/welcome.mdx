When you call Powered Up LLC, you get me — Micah. Same person who shows up to do
the work, runs the permit, and stands behind it after. This blog is an extension
of that: a place to share the kind of straight answers I'd give you on a service
call, without the call.

## What you'll find here

I'll keep it practical and local to the South Shore:

- **Panel upgrades** — when 100A isn't enough anymore, and what a 200A or 400A
  service actually buys you.
- **EV chargers** — how Level 2 installs work, load calculations, and what to
  check before you buy a charger.
- **Older homes** — knob-and-tube, aluminum wiring, Federal Pacific and Zinsco
  panels, and why your insurance carrier keeps asking about them.
- **Safety** — the warning signs that a tripping breaker or warm outlet is
  telling you something serious.

## Why this matters

A lot of electrical advice online is written to sell you something. My goal is
simpler: help you understand what's going on in your home so you can make a good
call — whether that's hiring me or just knowing the right question to ask.
